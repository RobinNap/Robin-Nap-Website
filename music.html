Music

A chapter closed, but never forgotten

After years of making music professionally, I've decided to step back from my career as a DJ and producer. What started as pure joy — getting goosebumps from crazy sounds alone in my room — had become something else entirely. Music became pressure instead of passion.

Combined with managing epilepsy since 2018, continuing the lifestyle I had pre-COVID wasn't sustainable. But I'm endlessly grateful for every moment — playing in different countries at a young age is something I'll remember forever.
